About Dabs Ltd.

Dab's Repair Ltd was established in 1999, starting with only 1 mechanic. Now over 20 years later Dabs Repair Ltd is now considered one of the best and most respected truck repair services in Winnipeg. With the recent addition to becoming the first Truck Pro dealer in Winnipeg we pride ourselves in offering an outstanding service experience unlike no other. Dabs Repair Ltd has now grown to employ over 10 men and women. Open from 7:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday thru Friday we are able to offer customers great service to make sure your trucks back on the road as soon as possible.

Moving into a larger shop in June of 2008 has only increased the benefit for all company needs, as well as owner operators. With the addition of becoming a Kleenoil, Lubecorp, Espar dealers. Dabs Repair Ltd is known for its outstanding service and providing top quality repairs to all of our clients. We know the importance when it comes to having your trucks in perfect shape and back on the road as soon as possible. You will always benefit from a high level of expertise, experience and professionalism that Dabs Repair Ltd stands by. We will continuously look for products that protect, save and enhance your driving experience.

2012 we became a TruckPro dealer in the area. New pricing and great national warranty are only some of the benefits.

So stop in at 2126 Logan Ave or call us and see how we can help you get back on the road and help you save time and money.
